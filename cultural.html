Face-Off

RULES

  1. Track will be provided on stage.
  2. No of participants on stage can be 2 to compete with each other
  3. Each participant will be given an initial time limit of 25-30 seconds to showtheir best dance skills on stage to pump up the fellow participants.
  4. The song continues as next participant performs while the first participant is in freezeposition for the next 25-30 seconds.
  5. Climax begins with all the participants dancing to the same continued song of same choreographed steps so that judgment can be made on synchronization and clarityof steps comparing each participant with one another to select the best.
  6. No usage of any kind of props.
  7. Stage cleanliness and discipline should be maintained.
  8. The decision of the Organizing Committee is final & participants must abide by it.
