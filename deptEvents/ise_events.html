QUEST-MANIA

This is a team event where players have multiple hurdles to tackle. The game consists of four rounds and elimination round begins from second round. Each round is filled with various fun games and challenging technical tasks. Each team must decode the hint given to them to win the top spot in each round. Embark on a tech-filled adventure and conquer challenges of Puzzles, Scribble, Code Roulette, and more in the ultimate Quest for Tech Glory.

RULES

  1. Undergraduate and Postgraduate students are allowed.
  2. Team Size is 2.
  3. Elimination Round begins from 2nd Round
  4. The participant must carry ID proof to show he/she is a UG/PG student.
  5. The participant should arrive the task area 15 mins early.
  6. Use of mobile/phone during the game is not allowed, if found team will be disqualified immediately.
  7. The decision of organizing committee is final.
  8. Participants disobeying the rules will be disqualified.

PAPER PRESENTATION

Paper presentation is an individual competition where each participant is required to make a paper about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of the juries. The contest is open for B.E/B.Tech and Polytechnic level students.

RULES

  1. Your work should be original and in the specified format.
  2. Interested candidates are advised to mail the complete paper in IEEE format and presentation in .PPT or .PPTX format.
  3. The competition is open to all Engineering /Diploma students.
  4. Students are requested to send their papers on isepaperpresentation2022@gmail.com
  5. The participants of shortlisted candidates will be informed by E-Mail ID or by Mobile.
  6. The selected teams have to bring a soft copy of paper in USB Device and 2 hard copies of paper at the time of presentation.
  7. Decision of the judges and the event heads shall be treated as final and binding on all and cannot be contested.
  8. Directly copying from the Internet is strongly discouraged and will not be entertained.
  9. All the candidates will get participation certificates.
  10. The participants should come with their college ID Card.
  11. Spot Entries are also accepted.
  12. For any clarification or further details the interested candidates can contact the Faculty or Student coordinators.
  13. Jury decision will be final decision.
